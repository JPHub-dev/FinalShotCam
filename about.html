Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinalShoot really free? +

Yes! FinalShoot is completely free to use. No hidden charges, no premium features locked behind paywalls. Simply capture, edit, and share your moments without spending a single penny.

What filters are available? +

FinalShoot offers a variety of creative filters including Retro, Pixel, Clay, Crayon, Cartoon, and many more. Each filter transforms your photos into unique artistic styles while maintaining their original charm.

Do I need to install anything? +

No installation required! FinalShoot is a web-based application that runs directly in your browser. Just visit the website, and you're ready to start capturing and editing.

Can I download my photos? +

Absolutely! After editing your photos with FinalShoot's filters, you can download them in high quality directly to your device. Share them on social media or keep them as cherished memories.

Is my data secure? +

Your privacy is our priority. All photos are processed locally in your browser. We don't store your images on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them.

What devices are supported? +

FinalShoot works on all modern devices - desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones. As long as you have a modern web browser, you can access FinalShoot anywhere, anytime.
